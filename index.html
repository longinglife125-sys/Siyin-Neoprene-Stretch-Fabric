Step-by-Step Excellence

Our Manufacturing Process

Transparency is our commitment. Here is how we build high-quality materials from scratch to ship.

Step 01

Rubber Splitting

Splitting large SBR/CR blocks into exact target thickness sheets (1.0mm to 20mm).

Step 02

Fabric Lamination

Applying custom functional fabrics to rubber using eco-friendly pur hot-melt glues.

Step 03

Custom Coating

Adding embossing, anti-slip finishes, or perforated holes as per order requirement.

Step 04

Strict Inspection

Rigorous quality checks for elasticity, thickness tolerance, and lamination strength.

Everything you need to know about starting an order with us.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For in-stock base neoprene sheets, the MOQ is just 10 sheets. For custom colored lamination or dyed stretch textiles, the MOQ is 500 meters per custom color.
