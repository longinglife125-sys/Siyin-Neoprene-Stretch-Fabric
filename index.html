Easy Customization

How to Customize Your Materials?

No complex jargon. Build your bespoke material in 3 simple steps.

📷 [Image: Raw Neoprene Slabs]
Step 01

Choose Core & Thickness

Select your rubber base (SBR for bags, SCR for entry wetsuits, or premium CR for professional diving) from 1.0mm to 10mm.

📷 [Image: Colored Fabric Rolls]
Step 02

Choose Fabric Lamination

Laminate your rubber with high-stretch Lycra, durable Nylon, budget Polyester, or medical-grade hook-compatible OK cloth.

📷 [Image: Embossed/Perforated Detail]
Step 03

Add Functional Finishes

Optimize your material with custom surface embossing (anti-slip sharkskin), ventilation perforation holes, or custom prints.
