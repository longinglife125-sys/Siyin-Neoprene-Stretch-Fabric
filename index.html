Our Manufacturing Focus

Neoprene & Lamination Engineering

  • Rapid Dispatch Program: Ready-to-ship inventory of high-grade SBR, SCR, and CR sheets/rolls to match tight schedules.
  • Technical Fabric Bonding: Perfect hot-melt PUR lamination with Lycra, Nylon Jersey, OK fabric, Mesh, and heavy Oxford.
  • Precision Treatments: Highly stable pattern embossing, custom punching (perforation), and foil stamping.
  • Streamlined Product Integrations: Fast-turnaround OEM manufacturing for mousepads, bottle koozies, and laptop sleeves.
